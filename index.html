Frequently Asked Questions

Everything UK freelancers ask before switching to InvoiceFlow.

Is InvoiceFlow really free? ▼
Yes — completely free during our launch period. No credit card, no trial, no hidden fees. You get unlimited invoices, proposals, clients, PDF exports, and reports at no cost. Pricing may be introduced in future and we'll give plenty of notice.
Do I need to create an account or sign up? ▼
No sign-up required at all. Your data is saved automatically in your browser. Just open the app and start creating. Your invoices, clients, and settings are there every time you come back.
Can I add VAT to my invoices? ▼
Yes. InvoiceFlow automatically calculates VAT at whatever rate you set (standard 20%, reduced 5%, or zero-rated 0%). The VAT amount and total are clearly shown on both the invoice editor and the PDF export.
Can I create client proposals as well as invoices? ▼
Yes — proposals are fully built in. You can add a project description, deliverables list, timeline milestones, pricing breakdown, and payment terms. Proposals export as professional PDFs with a branded cover page, just like invoices.
Is InvoiceFlow suitable for UK self-employed and sole traders? ▼
Absolutely. InvoiceFlow is built specifically for UK freelancers and sole traders. It handles GBP (£), UK VAT rates, and includes fields for your bank sort code and account number in the payment notes — exactly what UK clients expect to see.
Can I download invoices as PDF? ▼
Yes, one click downloads a professionally formatted PDF invoice or proposal. The PDF includes your branding, client details, line items, VAT breakdown, total, and payment notes — ready to email directly to your client.
Can I track which invoices have been paid? ▼
Yes. Every invoice has a status — Draft, Pending, Paid, or Overdue. Mark invoices as paid with one click. Your dashboard and reports automatically update to show total revenue, outstanding amounts, and monthly income charts.
What invoice templates are available? ▼
Four invoice templates: Modern Gradient (bold indigo-to-cyan header), Minimal (clean lines), Bold Dark (dark professional header), and Classic (traditional centred layout). Four proposal templates: Gradient Cover, Dark Professional, Minimal White, and Warm Sunset. Switch any time from the Templates page.
